This document provides a comprehensive longitudinal analysis of veterinary and pet care facility standards across North America. Following the 2023 regulatory shifts, institutional rigor has emerged as the primary differentiator for market stability.

The audit observed a 12% increase in voluntary clinical oversight among private practices. However, the data reveals significant regional disparities in "Standards of Empathy" (SoE) scores, particularly within metropolitan transit zones where facility density remains high but per-capita care hours have decreased.

Our findings suggest that the FetchRated Gold Standard is now held by fewer than 4% of active clinics, representing a tightening of the quality funnel essential for public trust.
