Technical Protocol v4.2: The Audit Framework

This document outlines the rigorous clinical and technical procedures used to verify pet-care institutions within the FetchRated Registry. It establishes the gold standard for operational integrity in the modern veterinary ecosystem.

01 Procedural Audit Requirements

1

Initial Institutional Intake

Verification of legal standing and local licensing via regional clinical databases. Institutions must provide 36 months of incident-free records.

  • check_circle Formal verification of Registered Veterinary Surgeons (RVS).
  • check_circle Validation of professional liability insurance minimums (£5M).
2

Facility Inspection Protocol

On-site audit involving 142 distinct data points across three domains: Hygiene, Safety, and Technological Infrastructure.

3

Post-Audit Review

Final deliberation by the Clinical Deanery before issuance of the FetchRated Institutional Badge.
