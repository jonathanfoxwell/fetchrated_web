The Qualification Gate

A rigorous three-stage verification process ensuring every practice meets the FetchRated benchmark for clinical excellence and ethical welfare.

01.

Submission

Initial data ingestion of practice credentials, professional indemnity coverage, and senior clinical staff certifications for the current cohort year.

02.

Clinical Audit

A remote-first audit of treatment outcomes, surgical suite standards, and patient recovery protocols reviewed by our peer committee.

03.

Trust Mark Issuance

Granting of the FetchRated Physical Seal and digital credentials, allowing public placement on the National Integrated Practice Register.

Assessment Protocol

Our assessment criteria are built upon the 2024 Welfare Framework. Practices must demonstrate compliance across four primary axes to achieve FetchRated accreditation.
